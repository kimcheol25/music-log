Trot History

Trot is one of Korea’s oldest pop genres, originating in the early 1900s. With unique vocal techniques, rhythmic patterns, and emotional expression, it has become a cultural symbol.

Across decades, legendary singers like Nam Jin and Na Hoon-a shaped its golden era. After a period of decline, modern TV shows revived trot’s popularity for younger generations.

Today, trot is experiencing a renaissance, blending traditional sounds with modern styles.
